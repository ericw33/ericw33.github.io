Our Story

Colleen and Eric met in November of 2012. They met through a mutual friend, Brittany, who introduced them. Eric agreed to try out Yoga with Brittany. It was during November, so Eric was doing "Movember". That meant he had a half-grown, very creepy-looking mustache. Colleen ended up not making the yoga session, but they met at brunch and had an amazing conversation. They met again at Brittany's holiday party a month later. Eric asked Colleen out for a yoga date to make up for the last one. However, Yoga turned into happy hour, there were many more dates, and Eric never did Yoga again.
